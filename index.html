     What Do We Do?

     We are Pulso Home Health LLC, a native El Paso agency dedicated to delivering medical care at a person's home. Our staff personnel work as a team to provide the most sophisticated and affordable treatment possible for our clients. We work with our patients personally, accompanied by his/her family and our professional colleagues. Therapists can administer a wide range of treatments, from physical to speech therapy. We hope you will join our Pulso community!

    In light of COVID-19, we have significantly increased precautions when providing any medical services. Please practice social distancing guidelines and remain at home during quarantine. Older adults and people with underlying medical conditions should adhere to COVID-19 regulations and take further precautions to protect themselves and those around them. If you wish to receive our services, we will conduct a preliminary check to ensure both the safety of our employees and patients. Thank you for your understanding.
