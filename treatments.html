      What treatments and services do you offer?         

    We administer a variety of treatments suited to your individual needs. Our range of services includes physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, skilled nursing, and home health aid.

    Also, we work closely with clients to provide optimal medical care and results at home. Our physical therapy services aim to improve movement or ability, reover from an injury after a stroke, accident, or surgery, and learn to use assistive devices such as a walker or cane.

    With these services, Pulso aims to provide convenient at-home medical care and support. We hope our patients become as self-sufficient as possible.

Occupational Therapy 

Through occupational therapy, patients will participate in self-care, work, and leisure activities to enhance their development and independence, or to prevent disability.

Speech Therapy

We also have dedicated speech therapists who treat speech impairments, including swallowing or communication disorders. Speech therapy services focus on improving one's communication.

Home Health Aid

We administer services directly at the patient's home. Especially for the elderly or disabled, we assist patients for dressing, bathing, and other daily activities.
