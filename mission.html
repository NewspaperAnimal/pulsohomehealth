      More About Us   

    As a home health agency, Pulso is committed to providing quality
in-home care. We personally provide efficient and cost-effective treatments without the need to visit a hospital. Our main goal is to help you regain your independence. We also administer non-medical care, such as assistance with eating, drinking, bathing, exercise, laundry, housecleaning duties, toileting, and many other daily activites.

    We are a local El Paso business, founded in 2012. Although we are based in El Paso, we offer are services to any person eligible for home health services. Pulso is willing to accept anyone regardless of race, ethnicity, nationality, or gender. Our mission is to give home health care and services to all those in need.
